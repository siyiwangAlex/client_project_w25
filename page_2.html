Physical Health

At the University of Michigan, we are dedicated to supporting our students' physical health and well-being by providing comprehensive resources and facilities.

Our University Health Service (UHS) offers convenient medical care and wellness support tailored to the specific needs of students, helping you address routine healthcare needs and any health concerns during your academic journey.

Additionally, the My UofM Health System provides an online platform to easily access your health records, schedule medical appointments.

Students can also take advantage of our extensive fitness facilities at the Recreation Building, equipped with modern exercise equipment, diverse group fitness classes.

Furthermore, our specialized Xgroup program guides students toward exercising scientifically, providing professional advice, personalized fitness plans.
