Mental Health

At the University of Michigan, we recognize the importance of mental health and emotional well-being in our students' academic and personal journeys.

Our Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS) provides confidential, professional support to help you manage stress, navigate emotional challenges, and maintain mental wellness throughout your studies.

In addition, our partnership with UWill offers telehealth therapy services.

UM students also benefit from personalized support through individual and peer counseling, creating opportunities for meaningful conversations.

Furthermore, the Rackham Graduate School Mental Health Supporting System specifically addresses the mental health needs of graduate students, providing resources, workshops.
