Life Outside Classroom

At the University of Michigan, we believe that a successful academic journey is deeply connected to students' well-being outside the classroom.

Our campus offers comprehensive support for essential needs, including assistance with housing, parking, and food security resources to help you manage everyday life with confidence.

The Maize & Blue Boards and other food access programs ensure that all students have access to nutritious meals.

The Center for the Education of Women+ (CEW+) provides tailored support for students of all genders and backgrounds, especially those navigating nontraditional paths in higher education.

In addition, a wide range of student organizations and campus groups offer opportunities to build community, develop leadership skills, and engage with shared interests.
